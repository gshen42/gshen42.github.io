Hi, I'm Gan Shen. I'm a third-year PhD student in Computer Science at University of California Santa Cruz, working with Prof. Lindsey Kuper in the LSD Lab.

My research area is programming languages theory. I'm interested in understanding the principles of programming languages, their connections to formal logic, and how to design new programming abstractions to facilitate software development.

I was an intern at Amazon Web Service in the Automated Reasoning Group.

My first name “Gan” is a single Chinese character “赣” which is the abbreviation for Jiangxi province of China, my mother's hometown.
