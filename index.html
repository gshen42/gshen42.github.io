About

Hi, I'm Gan Shen, a third-year Ph.D. student studying Computer Science at the University of California, Santa Cruz. I'm a member of the Languages, Systems, and Data (LSD) Lab and my advisor is Lindsey Kuper.

My research area lies within programming languages, with a focus on building correct, safe, and fast concurrent/distributed systems with language-based approaches. More specifically, I work on choreographic programming, which is a paradigm where the programmers write a distributed system as a single, unified program — called choreographies — and then compile it to individual programs that run on each node.

I was an intern in the Automated Reasoning Group at Amazon Web Service in the summer of 2022, where I worked on modeling and verifying distributed protocols.
